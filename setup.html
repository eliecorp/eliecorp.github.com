To further improve our communication with you, we are implementing new technology that will help us to be more organized and efficient in the way we all work together.

As always, reach out if you have any questions or need assistance in the setup process.

 

Slack - Even better communication.

We are switching over real-time communication to Slack!

 

It IS the professional real-time communication platform. Slack Lets you:

-search messages even when you’re not connected to the internet

-initiate a Google Hangout with the click of a button

-play/download mp3’s and videos from the chat

 

Text and Email Are absolutely still fine for their respective purposes, but we recommend switching over from Facebook to Slack for our instant messaging.

Steps:

1)    Install Slack for your Mac Here

2)    Watch our guide on setting up Slack

3)    Watch our video on using Slack

 

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       

 

Workflowy - Even better transparency and productivity.

to ensure everybody knows what they are assigned and what everyone on the team is doing overall, we are sharing our favorite task manager Workflowy .

 

Workflowy is a simple but powerful collaborative to-do list. We have been testing it internally for 6 months and have found it to be extremely beneficial.

 

 

SignNow - Even Better Turnaround Time.

 

SignNow will allow you to sign documents from your phone so you no longer have to print, sign, and scan.

Instead, we can send you a pre-marked document with instructions on where you need to sign so you can approve and sign on-the-go.

(We still 100% encourage you to read through contracts before signing them! This just makes it easier to actually sign them.)

We will not offer this service for contracts between ECorp and you. This is more for buyer-signed contracts for venues and the like.

 

Steps:

1)          Install SignNow on your Android or iPhone from their respective app stores. To do so, search for “SignNow” and look for this logo:

2)          Open the app, create an account with your primary email address

3)          Open the verification email you receive shortly after creating an account

4)          Go back into the app and log in

5)          You’re done and ready for us to send you documents to sign!
