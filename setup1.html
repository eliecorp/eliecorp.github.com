The third big update is Even quicker turnaround time (even when you’re on the road).

 

SignNow will allow you to sign documents from your phone so you no longer have to print, sign, and scan.

Instead, we can send you a pre-marked document with instructions on where you need to sign so you can approve and sign on-the-go.

(We still 100% encourage you to read through contracts before signing them! This just makes it easier to actually sign them.)

We will not offer this service for contracts between ECorp and you. This is more for buyer-signed contracts for venues and the like.

 

Steps:

1)          Install SignNow on your Android or iPhone from their respective app stores. To do so, search for “SignNow” and look for this logo:

2)          Open the app, create an account with your primary email address

3)          Open the verification email you receive shortly after creating an account

4)          Go back into the app and log in

5)          You’re done and ready for us to send you documents to sign!
